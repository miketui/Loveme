## Chapter Title Parsing Rules

### Title Line Breaking Strategy
Break long chapter titles into vertical lines for visual impact:

**Example: "MASTERING THE BUSINESS OF HAIRSTYLING"**
```xml
<div class="title-line">MASTERING</div>
<div class="title-line">THE</div>
<div class="title-line">BUSINESS</div>
<div class="title-line">OF</div>
<div class="title-line">HAIRSTYLING</div>
```

**Parsing Rules:**
1. Break at natural word boundaries
2. Aim for 3-6 lines maximum
3. Maintain visual balance
4. Keep related words together when possible
5. Consider line length for mobile devices
